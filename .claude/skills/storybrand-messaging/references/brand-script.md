Complete this worksheet to create your brand's core messaging. Use the answers to write all marketing materials.

## Validation Checklist

Before using this brand script, verify:

- [ ] Character wants something specific (not vague)
- [ ] Internal problem is identified (feelings, not just facts)
- [ ] You demonstrate empathy AND authority (not just one)
- [ ] Plan has 3-4 steps maximum (not a detailed process)
- [ ] Direct CTA is clear and specific
- [ ] Success paints a desirable picture
- [ ] Failure creates stakes (without being manipulative)
- [ ] One-liner can be repeated after hearing once
